## Professional Summary

I am a **Research Scientist** at **Acuity Knowledge Partners**, specializing in **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and **AI-driven financial automation**. With a **Dual Degree (Ms+Ph.D)** from the **Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati**, I bring extensive research experience in speech processing, deep learning, and natural language understanding.

My work spans developing AI agents for investment research, building multilingual embedding models, implementing RLHF alignment techniques, and creating production-grade conversational AI systems.
